# Privacy Policy

## In Short
Buzz Grades is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when using our website.

## Collecting Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from users, except for anonymized usage data, including
information about how you interact with Buzz Grades, such as page visits and navigation actions.

When reporting a bug, we may collect your email address solely for providing feedback or clarifying the issue you have reported.
Your email address is not used for any other purposes, including sharing your email address with third parties.

## Using Information

We collect anonymized usage data to enhance user experience on Buzz Grades, which helps us understand
how users interact with this website and identify areas for improvement. It is for solely this purpose of communicating
the issue that we collect your email address. In addition, we may ask for additional details, clarification, or provide updates
on the bug you reported, but the email address will not be shared with third parties.

## Third-Party Services

Buzz Grades may contain links to external websites such as Rate My Professor that are not owned by Buzz Grades.
We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites, and this privacy policy only applies to Buzz Grades.

## Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this privacy policy at any time, and any policy changes will be effective
immediately upon updating the website. It is therefore your responsibility to stay up-to-date with this privacy policy
on how we collect, use, and protect your information.
